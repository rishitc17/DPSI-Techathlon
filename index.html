About

Where empathy becomes innovation.

DPS International School, Gurugram proudly presents Techathlon — the school's annual flagship technology event. Now in its 4th annual edition in 2026, Techathlon has grown each year since 2023, bringing together students to innovate, collaborate, and solve meaningful real-world problems.

Participants investigate authentic challenges, understand diverse perspectives, and design solutions that improve everyday experiences across education, healthcare, accessibility, sustainability, digital wellbeing, and community development.
